Tacos

Description

These classic tacos are packed with seasoned ground beef, fresh lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese, all wrapped in a warm tortilla. Perfect for a quick and delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells or tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream and salsa (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the taco seasoning to the beef according to the package instructions. Simmer for a few minutes.
  3. Warm the taco shells or tortillas in the oven or microwave.
  4. Fill each shell or tortilla with the seasoned beef, lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese.
  5. Top with sour cream and salsa if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy!
